Traditional Cypriot meze is one of the great dining experiences on the island — 20+ dishes, lots of halloumi, grilled meats, and dips. But prices vary wildly. Here's where to get the best bang for your euro.

## Nicosia

| Restaurant | Meze Price (per person) | Notes |
|------------|------------------------|-------|
| **Stou Kir Yianni** | €15 | Classic old-town meze, generous portions |
| **Meze tou Voskou** | €17 | Traditional village-style setting |
| **Kathodon** | €18 | Popular with locals, great grills |
| **To Anamma** | €22 | Slightly upscale but excellent quality |

**Best value in Nicosia: Stou Kir Yianni** at €15/person — a true Nicosia institution.
