The Electricity Authority of Cyprus (EAC) is the sole electricity provider in Cyprus, so you cannot switch supplier. However, **choosing the right tariff** can significantly reduce your bill.

## EAC Residential Tariffs (2026)

| Tariff | Fixed Charge | Unit Rate | Best For |
|--------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| **Domestic (D1)** | €2.50/month | €0.1723/kWh | Low usage households |
| **Domestic Economy (D2)** | €3.00/month | Day: €0.1723 / Night: €0.0969/kWh | Homes with flexible usage |
| **Domestic Time-of-Use (DTOU)** | €3.50/month | Peak: €0.2100 / Off-peak: €0.0850/kWh | Smart meter households |

## Which Tariff Saves the Most?

### Low usage (under 300 kWh/month)
**D1 is best** — simple, no fixed charge premium, predictable billing.

### Medium usage (300–600 kWh/month)
**D2 (Economy)** — if you can run dishwasher, washing machine, and EV charging overnight (midnight–6am), the night rate of €0.0969/kWh vs €0.1723 saves ~44% on those loads.

### High usage with solar/EV
**DTOU** — off-peak rate of €0.0850/kWh is excellent for overnight EV charging and battery storage discharge.

## Tips to Reduce Your EAC Bill

1. **Switch to D2 tariff** — free to do, call EAC or apply online. Saves up to €17/month for a family.
2. **Run appliances at night** — washing machines, dishwashers, and EV chargers are cheapest midnight–6am on D2.
3. **Solar panels** — payback period is 5–7 years in Cyprus due to high sunshine hours.
4. **LED lighting** — a full house switch saves approximately €5–10/month.
5. **Smart thermostat** — AC is the #1 electricity cost in Cyprus summers. A smart thermostat can cut AC bills by 15–20%.

## How to Switch Tariff

1. Call EAC on **77778000** or visit eac.com.cy
2. Request a tariff change — it takes effect from next billing cycle
3. No fees or equipment required for D1→D2
