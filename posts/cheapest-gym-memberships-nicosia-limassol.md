## Tips

- **Annual memberships** save 20–35% vs paying monthly — if you know you'll stick with it, commit upfront
- Most gyms offer a **free trial day** — always try before you sign
- **January and September** are the best months to negotiate — gyms are hungry for members
- Some gyms offer **student discounts** of 10–20% with a valid student card
