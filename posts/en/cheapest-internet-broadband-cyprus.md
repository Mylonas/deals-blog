## Tips for Getting a Better Deal

1. **New customer deals** — Epic and Cyta regularly offer 3–6 months free for new subscribers. Always check the website before signing up.
2. **Negotiate at renewal** — threaten to switch and existing customers often get a retention discount of €5–10/month.
3. **Check fibre availability first** — fibre plans offer much better value per Mbps. Not all areas are covered yet.
4. **Bundle vs standalone** — if you need TV, a bundle is usually cheaper than buying separately.
