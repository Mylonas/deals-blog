Whether you're at UCY, CUT, Frederick, or any other Cyprus university, these are the discounts every student in Cyprus should know about.

## 🚌 Transport

### 1. OSEA Buses — 50% Off All Routes (University Students)
University and higher education students get **50% off all OSEA and Pancyprian bus tickets** with a **Discount Motion Smart Card**.

| Who qualifies | How to get it |
|--------------|---------------|
| University/college students in Cyprus or abroad | Apply at OSEA offices with your student ID |
| European Youth Card holders | Bring your EYC card |

**Cost of regular ticket:** €1.50 → **Student: €0.75**

### 2. OSEA Buses — €5/Month (Secondary School Students)
Secondary school students pay just **€5/month or €30/year** for unlimited travel (government subsidised — real cost is €22/month).

### 3. Intercity Buses — Student Rate
Intercity buses between Nicosia, Limassol, Larnaca and Paphos offer student rates. Ask at the counter with a valid student ID.

## 💻 Software & Tech

### 4. Microsoft Office 365 — Free
All students at UCY, CUT, Frederick, and most Cyprus universities get **Microsoft 365 (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Teams, OneDrive) completely free** via their university email. Log in at office.com with your .ac.cy email.

### 5. Adobe Creative Cloud — ~60% Off
Adobe offers a **student & teacher plan at ~€19.99/month** (vs €54.99 standard) — that's 60%+ off Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere, and the full Creative Suite. Verify with your .ac.cy email at adobe.com/education.

### 6. GitHub Student Developer Pack — Free (~€200+ value)
GitHub's **Student Developer Pack** gives you free access to:
- GitHub Pro (private repos, advanced tools)
- JetBrains IDEs (PyCharm, IntelliJ, WebStorm etc.) — free while enrolled
- Namecheap domain — 1 year free
- Canva Pro — free
- MongoDB Atlas credits, and 100+ more tools

Apply at education.github.com/pack with your .ac.cy email.

### 7. JetBrains IDEs — Free for Students
All JetBrains tools (PyCharm, IntelliJ, WebStorm, DataGrip, etc.) are **free for students** via the GitHub Student Pack or directly at jetbrains.com/student.

### 8. Canva Pro — Free for Students
Canva Pro (normally €12.99/month) is **free for students** via the GitHub Student Pack or directly at canva.com/education.

### 9. Apple Education Store — Up to 10% Off + Free AirPods
Apple offers **education pricing** on Mac and iPad:
- MacBook Air/Pro: save €50–€120
- iPad: save €30–€50
- **Free AirPods** with qualifying Mac or iPad purchase during back-to-school season (usually June–September)

Shop at apple.com/education or visit iStorm Cyprus (Apple Premium Reseller).

## 🎬 Entertainment

### 10. K-Cineplex — Student Price €6.50
Present a valid student ID at any K-Cineplex in Cyprus for a **student ticket at €6.50** (vs €8.00 standard). Valid any day.

### 11. Odeon Cinema — Tuesday Deal €5.00
Odeon cinemas offer **€5.00 tickets every Tuesday** — no student ID needed. For students, this is the cheapest way to catch a new release.

### 12. European Youth Card — 55 Businesses in Cyprus (Free)
The **European Youth Card (EYC)** is issued **free of charge** to anyone aged 13–30 in Cyprus by the Cyprus Youth Organisation (ONEK). It gives discounts at 55+ businesses including:
- Theatres (Satiriko, Theatro Skala, Theatro Anemona, ETHAL)
- Cyprus Symphony Orchestra
- Fasouri Watermania Waterpark
- Hard Rock Café Ayia Napa
- Hotels, restaurants, shops and more

Apply at onek.org.cy — free for all young people under 30.

### 13. Cyprus Museums — Free or Discounted Entry
Most state museums in Cyprus (including the Cyprus Museum in Nicosia) offer **free or heavily discounted entry for students** with a valid student ID or EYC card.

### 14. Leventis Museum (Nicosia) — Free Entry
The Leventis Municipal Museum of Nicosia is **free for everyone**, including students. Great for history and culture.

Most gyms in Cyprus offer student discounts when you show a valid student card:
- **Fit Factory:** €25 → ~€20–22 with student card
- **Anytime Fitness:** 10% student discount
- Ask at the desk — not always advertised

### 16. Gloria Jean's Coffees — EYC Discount
With your **European Youth Card**, get discounts at Gloria Jean's Coffees locations in Cyprus.

### 17. Amazon Prime Student — 50% Off
Amazon Prime Student is available in Cyprus at approximately **€2.49/month** (50% off regular price) for the first 6 months free trial, then half price. Includes free delivery, Prime Video, and Prime Music.

## 🎓 Academic & Learning

### 18. Spotify Premium — ⚠️ NOT Available in Cyprus
Spotify **does not currently offer a student plan in Cyprus**. The standard Premium plan in Cyprus costs €9.99/month with no student discount. This is a known gap — vote for it on Spotify's community forum.

### 19. Google Workspace for Education — Free via University
Most Cyprus universities provide **Google Workspace (Gmail, Drive, Docs, Meet)** free via your university account. Check with your IT department.

### 20. ISIC Card — International Student Identity Card
The **ISIC card** (€15/year) is the internationally recognised student ID that unlocks discounts at thousands of businesses worldwide, including:
- Travel discounts (flights, trains, hotels)
- Software and tech
- Entertainment

Apply at isic.org — accepted globally and at many Cyprus businesses.
